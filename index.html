Instructions

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso or simply Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards

Why should I play?

Aside from being fun to play, this game can help improve your memory and concentration. Memory games have been scientifically proven to help keep your brain healthy as you age. So enjoy playing and stay sharp!
